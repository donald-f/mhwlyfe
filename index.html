Earplugs Effect

Earplugs is increased in level based on the amount of Equipment with this skill the hunter is using. Each level works as follows:

  • Level 1: Slightly reduces the effects of weak monster roars
  • Level 2: Reduces the effects of weak monster roars
  • Level 3: Nullifies weak monster roars
  • Level 4: Nullifies weak monster roars and reduces the effects of strong monster roars
  • Level 5: Nullifies weak and strong monster roars
